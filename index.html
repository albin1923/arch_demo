The Studio

Noxus Studio is defined by an unwavering commitment to absolute minimalism. We shape spaces that evoke calm and clarity through the essentialist reduction of form — stripping away the superfluous to leave only what is truly necessary.

Founded in 2018, we operate at the intersection of architecture, interior design, and material innovation. Every project begins with a singular question: what does the space want to become?
